6-Minute Strength Routine

Here are some simple strength exercises you can do at home:

  • Abdominal contractions
  • Wall pushups
  • Pelvic tilts
  • Shoulder blade squeeze
  • Toe taps
  • Heel raises
  • Knee lifts
  • Shoulder and upper back stretch
  • Ankle rotations

Stretching Exercises

Getting into the habit of stretching every day will improve your range of motion. Here are two basic stretches to start with:

  • Neck stretch
  • Upper back stretch

Balance Boosters

Balance exercises are important for preventing falls. Here are a few you can try:

  • Shifting weight
  • Single leg balance
